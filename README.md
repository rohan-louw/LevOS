# LevOS

## Human Performance Operating System

LevOS is a data engineering and analytics project that transforms Apple Health wearable data into structured datasets for performance, recovery, readiness, and adaptation modelling.

---

## Vision

Most wearables describe the past.

# LevOS models the future.

LevOS creates a living digital twin from physiological, behavioral, and wearable data to understand human adaptation, determine current state, predict future performance outcomes, and guide better decisions over time.

---

## Mission

Transform human performance data into a living model that helps people understand themselves, make better decisions, train smarter, recover more effectively, and reach their full potential.

---

## Core Philosophy

**Understand. Predict. Adapt.**

- Understand your current state.
- Predict future outcomes.
- Adapt decisions and behavior accordingly.

---

- Apple Health XML extraction
- Data inventory generation
- ETL data pipelines
- Heart Rate Variability (HRV) processing
- Resting Heart Rate processing
- Sleep data processing
